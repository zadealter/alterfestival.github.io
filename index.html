About Alter

Alter Festival is a 3 day festival in Hampshire, near Salisbury, hosting a lineup of grassroots electronic music DJs from the minds behind Wax Palace and Aurora Sounds. We want to bring people together and create cohesion, in an increasingly online world which can be alienating, committed to curating a lineup which is fully representative of the electronic music community, with Female, NB and LGBTQ+ DJs as headliners, not just support acts.

We are crafting an experience which offers attendees an escape from the alienation of the modern world and fosters a sense of community, dedicated to making the festival a visual feast for our attendees to experience and lose themselves in, through spectacular staging and lighting design and interactive works of art. We will be focusing on creating art pieces aimed at visualising sound and light together as a medium, creating a space where your senses of the world (hearing, sight, touch) are explored in a relationship that wouldn’t usually occur.

Our aims are to curate a safe space for all, an affordable event that focuses on under-represented talent in an environment like no other. We hope to see you all in August!

FAQs

  • Location

    Alter Festival is located next to New Forest national park, 20 minutes south of Salisbury.

    Damerham, New Forest, Hampshire, England, SP6 3JN

  • Date

    Alter Festival will run from the 2nd August - 4th August, hosting music until late on Friday, Saturday and Sunday.

  • Travel

    The Festival is 2 hours from London and 1 ½ hours from Bristol by car, located 5 minutes from both the A354 and A338.

    The closest train station is Salisbury, we will be running a shuttle service which will be announced closer to the summer.

  • Camping

    Standard camping is included in the ticket price.

    You're welcome to bring a campervan.
